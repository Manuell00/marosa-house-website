Privacy & Cookie Policy

This page explains in a simple way how personal data and cookies are handled on the MaRoSa House website.

Contact details

Data controller

MaRoSa House. For privacy requests you can write to manuell.caselli@gmail.com.

Purpose of the data

Data sent through email, WhatsApp or stay requests is used only to answer booking enquiries and assistance requests.

Cookies

The site uses essential technical cookies to remember the cookie banner choice. If you accept, Google Analytics 4 is loaded to measure visits, page views and key interactions such as clicks on WhatsApp, Booking, Airbnb and stay enquiries. External services such as Google Maps or web fonts may also load their own resources.

Retention

Voluntarily shared data is kept only for the time needed to manage the request and any follow-up contact.

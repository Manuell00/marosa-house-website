How to get here

By train

Rapallo train station is only a few minutes from the apartments and has direct connections with Genoa, Milan and Cinque Terre.

By car

Rapallo is easy to reach via the A12 motorway. Recommended exit: Rapallo.

By plane

The nearest airport is Genoa Cristoforo Colombo, about 30 minutes away by car.

Parking

In the center of Rapallo you will find public parking areas and private garages close to the apartments.

Public parking

Paid public parking is available in the nearby streets.

Check-in and stay times

The arrival and departure rules are the same for all MaRoSa House apartments.

Check-in
